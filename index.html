Horizon

Horizon helps fleet enterprises and managers to save costs with the help of truck data analysis, data engineering, machine learning and a powerful front-end.

The Project

The software was developed during summer semester 2020 in the course "Data Science Project" at Stuttgart Media University under the direction of Professor Dr. Jan Kirenz and in cooperation with Adesso.

The Team

Our team consists of five developers with different background knowledge, experience and development skills.
